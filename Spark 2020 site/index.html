What is Real-Lax?

Real-Lax is a phone application available on IOS and Android. It offers three major features. It reminds the user to do certain positive healthy acts through a calendar. It teaches the user about mental health issues and how to combat them with various different lessons. It helps locate nearby professionals for further help or professional help. All of these features are put into a user-friendly application which can be utilized for free!

Who is Real-Lax for?

Real-Lax is a companion app designed for people suffering from stress leading to problems with their mental health. The age group recommended and prioritized for Real-Lax are working adults and university students, but people of all ages are welcomed to use the app to familiarize themselves with mental health issues. Real-Lax is optimal for reducing stress and anyone who feels like the pressures of work or school may be harming their mental health can download the app and utilize it for no cost whatsoever!

Why choose Real-Lax?

You also gain access to more in-depth lessons and get exclusive access to go through the newest lessons earlier than any free members. How does it all work?

Real-Lax provides the resources for people to manage their time, learn about mental health, and contact professionals. The University of Michigan points out that setting realistic goals to achieve can help and this is how our calendar function is very useful. Additionally seeking treatment from professionals is a great way to improve mental health, hence the seek help feature in Real-Lax. The overall palette was chosen as blue to bring the feeling of serenity and is seen as non-threatening, according to colour therapy blue also provides a calming atmosphere . The UI was made simple so that any user can quickly open and use the app. Real-Lax implements ads to help maintain the app and also offers a premium version which provides plenty of other features as well as beta testing of features. Real-Lax aims to ensure the peace of mind and positive mental health is available for anyone anywhere.
