Ruhaim Irshad Ali

Salutations my name is Ruhaim and I am currently attending Central Peel Secondary School. I am one of the co-founders of Real-Lax. As a busy student, I used to have difficulty getting time to care for my mental health and did not believe mental health was important. But after feeling the pain associated with poor mental health I decided to find the best ways to be mentally healthy. After spending countless hours I now understand the importance of mental health and how to improve my own mental health. The theme of this hackathon was wondrous and I was able to further my understanding on the topic of mind through a variety of ways, for example researching and playing an important role in the creation of our prototype app. The goal I had while creating Real-Lax was to provide others with knowledge to improve their mental health as I understand the struggles they may go through. Through Real-Lax I know how to assist and aid people, further my knowledge and improve my transferable skills, especially technological fluency. Overall, this has been an amazing experience and I am proud of Real-Lax!
